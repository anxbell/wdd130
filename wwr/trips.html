Class I - II

Float Trips

Float Trips Enjoy a relaxing float trip with minimal obstructions and small waves, perfect for families looking to make fun memories together.

  • Small waves
  • Easy navigation

Class III

Beginner Level

Class III Beginner Level Experience a wet and fun trip with some exciting obstacles, suitable for those with some technical skills looking for a challenge.

  • Well-trained guide required

Class IV

Intermediate Rafters

Navigate through big drops and whitewater with obstacles that require precise handling and more advanced skills.

  • Higher risk of capsizing
  • Complex maneuvering required
Book Trip

Advanced Rafters

Take on violent rapids and major obstacles on this extremely demanding route, only suitable for experienced rafters with expert skills.
